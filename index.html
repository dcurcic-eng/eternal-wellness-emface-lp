Facial Aesthetics

A Non-Invasive Alternative to Injectable Treatments

Emface uses synchronized radiofrequency and HIFES technology to stimulate collagen production, increase elastin fibers, and restore facial muscle tone — delivering a natural-looking lift without a single needle. The 20-minute treatment targets forehead lines, brow lift, cheek contouring, and overall skin tightening for a refreshed, youthful appearance.

Submentum Treatment

Redefine Your Jawline & Chin

Emface Submentum is a specialized application designed to target the area under the chin and along the jawline. By using HIFES to strengthen the platysma and digastric muscles beneath the chin, combined with RF energy to tighten overlying skin, this treatment contours the lower face and reduces the appearance of a double chin — all without surgery or injectables.

Beyond Aesthetics

Relief for TMJ & Jaw Tension

Emface is not only an aesthetic tool — it offers meaningful therapeutic benefits for patients suffering from TMJ (temporomandibular joint) disorder. The HIFES technology helps relax and rehabilitate overworked jaw muscles, reducing tension, pain, and clenching. Combined with radiofrequency, which promotes circulation and tissue healing, Emface provides a non-invasive, drug-free approach to TMJ management.

The Science

How Emface Works

01

HIFES Technology

High-Intensity Focused Electrical Stimulation selectively activates facial muscles, increasing muscle density and tone — much like a targeted workout for your face.

02

Radiofrequency Energy

Synchronized RF energy heats the dermal layer, stimulating the production of collagen, elastin, and hyaluronic acid to restore skin firmness and smoothness.

03

Combined Results

By integrating both technologies simultaneously, Emface lifts facial muscles from beneath while rejuvenating the skin above — producing a smoother, more youthful appearance with no downtime.
