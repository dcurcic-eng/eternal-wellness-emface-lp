The Science

How Emface Works

01

HIFES Technology

High-Intensity Focused Electrical Stimulation selectively activates facial muscles, increasing muscle density and tone. Think of it as a targeted workout for your face.

02

Radiofrequency Energy

Synchronized RF energy heats the dermal layer, stimulating the production of collagen, elastin, and hyaluronic acid to restore skin firmness and smoothness.

03

Combined Results

By integrating both technologies simultaneously, Emface lifts facial muscles from beneath while rejuvenating the skin above. The result is a smoother, more youthful appearance with no downtime.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Most patients describe the sensation as a warm, tingling feeling combined with gentle muscle contractions. The treatment is comfortable and requires no anesthesia. Many patients relax or even nap during their 20-minute session.

A typical treatment protocol consists of 4 sessions spaced 5–10 days apart. Results become visible after the first treatment and continue to improve for several weeks following the final session. Maintenance treatments every 6–12 months help sustain results.

There is zero downtime with Emface. You can return to your normal activities immediately after treatment. Some patients experience mild redness that typically subsides within an hour.

Emface is not recommended for individuals who are pregnant, have electronic implants (such as pacemakers or defibrillators), metal implants in the treatment area, or active skin conditions on the face. A consultation with our team will determine your eligibility.

Unlike Botox (which temporarily paralyzes muscles) or fillers (which add volume), Emface works by strengthening your own facial muscles and stimulating your body's natural collagen production. It's a completely needle-free approach that complements or can serve as an alternative to injectables.
